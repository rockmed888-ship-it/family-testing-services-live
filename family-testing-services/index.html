💅

Fingernail (Nail Keratin) Drug Testing — Our Specialty

Also known as nail matrix drug testing or keratinized nail specimen analysis, this method clips a small sample of fingernail (or toenail) and screens for drug metabolites embedded in the keratin. Detection window: up to 6 months. Non-invasive, difficult to adulterate, and ideal for custody cases, probation, and families who want a longer look-back without hair collection.

Turnaround: Specimens are sent to our COLA-accredited partner lab in Birmingham. Results typically return in 2 to 3 weeks.
